# Habit Definitions

This page defines the daily habits that are tracked in the Daily Notes.

---

# 1. Meditation

**Goal**

Practice mindful meditation for **5 minutes** using the Japanese sitting posture.

**Success Criteria**

- Completed at least 5 minutes.

---

# 2. Exercise

**Goal**

Complete the scheduled workout for the day.

**Success Criteria**

- Warm-up completed.
- Scheduled workout completed.
- Recovery or stretching completed (if applicable).

---

# 3. Book Reading

**Goal**

Read a book every day.

**Success Criteria**

- Read for at least 15–30 minutes or complete the planned reading session.

---

# 4. 1% Better

**Goal**

Do one meaningful action that improves yourself.

Examples

- Learn something new
- Improve a workflow
- Organize your workspace
- Solve a difficult problem
- Practice a skill
- Reflect and learn from mistakes

**Success Criteria**

Complete at least one action that contributes to long-term personal growth.
